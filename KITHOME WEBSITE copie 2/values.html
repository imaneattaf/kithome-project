Our Values

Vegan: none of our items are made from animals, which means that we offer, among other things, perfectly imitated vegan leathers and natural dyes.

Durable: Most of our products are made from recycled materials. In addition, our products can be returned when you no longer want them so that we can give them a second life

Local production: Our products are manufactured in France or in our workshops in Europe, Portugal or Spain in order to limit the transport and thus our environmental impact.
